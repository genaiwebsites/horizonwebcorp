# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
High-value businesses, founders, and product teams seeking a premium digital presence, experiential websites, and elite frontend performance.

## Product Purpose
Showcase Horizon Web Corp's craft (WebGL, custom animations, sub-second latency, elite design) to win projects, showcase featured client work, and convert prospective client inquiries.

## Brand Personality
* **Precision**: Meticulous attention to detail in layout, spacing, alignment, and performance.
* **Cold-Luxury**: Sophisticated, dark-tech aesthetic, subtle glows, and elegant transitions.
* **Technical Elegance**: High-end WebGL/Three.js integrated into clean semantic structures.

## Anti-references
* Warm-cream/sand-colored saturated SaaS templates.
* Cheap, fast-flickering, or bouncing "mosquito-like" motion.
* Cluttered dashboards or generic template grids.

## Design Principles
1. **Artistry Meets Engineering**: Every pixel and interaction must feel custom-designed, not auto-generated.
2. **Speed is the Baseline**: Fast load times, buttery smooth scrolling, and instant responsiveness.
3. **Restraint Over Clutter**: Motion and colors must be deliberate, purposeful, and quiet, never noisy or overstimulating.
4. **Legibility First**: Dark surfaces and background gradients must never compromise typography contrast and readability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* Targeting WCAG AA compliance with high-contrast copy against dark backgrounds.
* Full keyboard and screen reader accessibility on all interactive controls.
* Support for reduced motion preferences (`prefers-reduced-motion`).
